For Sponsors & CROs

Feasibility requests

Email your protocol or synopsis to hnhctc@hnhmgl.com. We will provide a detailed feasibility response within 5 business days including:

  • –Estimated screen failure rate and basis for that estimate
  • –Projected monthly enrolment and confidence level
  • –Realistic site activation timeline (IRB, CTA, SIV)
  • –Infrastructure fit — any missing equipment or referral dependencies
  • –PI interest and availability
For Physicians

Referring patients

If you have a patient who may be eligible for one of our current studies, please contact us with the patient's diagnosis, stage, and prior treatment history. We will arrange a prompt eligibility review and keep you fully informed of the outcome.

Referral letters can be sent to hnhctc@hnhmgl.com. All communications are handled confidentially.

For HCC, NSCLC, colorectal cancer, and breast cancer patients, please see the current study listings for detailed eligibility criteria before referring.
